Privacy Policy

The Grey Cell Brief (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website [www.thegreycellbrief.com] (the “Site”).

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit the Site, such as your IP address, browser type, referring URL, pages visited, and the date/time of your visit.

We may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the Site. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may limit some functionalities.

We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Your data may be stored on secure servers through our hosting and email service providers. We implement reasonable physical, administrative, and technical safeguards to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., Canada, EU, California), you may have these rights. To make a request, contact us at: info@thegreycellbrief.com
